Privacy Policy

Script Anchor is an application that runs locally on your device. We prioritize your privacy and security!

No Data Collection: Script Anchor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All interactions occur directly on your device without any involvement from any external servers or services owned by us.

Data Usage: Any data leaving your device is the result of your direct actions, such as connecting to other computers through SSH or REST interfaces. We do not monitor, store, or have access to these communications.

No Information Sharing: Since we do not collect any data, there is no information to share with third parties.

Security: Your use of Script Anchor is confined to your device, significantly reducing the risks associated with external data breaches or third-party data sharing.

Changes to This Policy: Should there be any future updates to these practices, they will be posted on this page.
